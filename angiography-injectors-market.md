<p><p>The angiography injectors market is divided into two main types: single-head and dual-head angiography injectors. Single-head injectors are designed to deliver contrast media for imaging tests through a single injection point, making them suitable for routine procedures. In contrast, dual-head injectors feature two separate injection heads that allow for simultaneous or sequential injections, enhancing efficiency and flexibility during complex imaging tasks. This differentiation helps cater to a variety of clinical needs and procedural requirements in radiology.</p></p>
