<p><p>The animal electric fence market is divided into two main types: permanent and portable fences. Permanent fences are designed for long-term use, typically featuring robust materials and infrastructure, making them ideal for large-scale livestock management. They provide a stable boundary that can withstand various environmental conditions. In contrast, portable fences are lightweight and easily adjustable, allowing for temporary enclosures and flexible grazing management. This type is particularly beneficial for rotational grazing and can be quickly set up or moved as needed.</p></p>
