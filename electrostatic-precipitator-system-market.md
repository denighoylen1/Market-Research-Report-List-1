<p><p>The Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP) market is primarily divided into two types: Wet ESP and Dry ESP. Wet ESPs use a liquid medium to capture and remove particulate matter from flue gases, making them effective in handling sticky or moist particles. In contrast, Dry ESPs operate without water, utilizing high-voltage electrical fields to attract and collect dry particles from gases, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Each type offers unique advantages based on specific operational needs and environmental requirements.</p></p>
