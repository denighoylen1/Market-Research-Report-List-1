<p><p>Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) involves various techniques to improve oil extraction beyond primary and secondary methods. CO2 Enhanced Oil Recovery utilizes injected carbon dioxide to reduce oil viscosity and increase pressure. Nitrogen Enhanced Oil Recovery uses nitrogen for similar purposes. Polymer Flooding involves adding polymers to increase water viscosity, aiding oil displacement. Surfactant Flooding uses surfactants to reduce surface tension, while Alkaline Flooding introduces alkaline substances to optimize oil recovery. Micellar Flooding employs micelles to enhance oil extraction, along with other emerging EOR methods.</p></p>
