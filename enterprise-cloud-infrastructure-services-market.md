<p><p>Enterprise Cloud Infrastructure Services encompass three primary market types: IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS. IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing businesses to rent servers and storage. PaaS (Platform as a Service) offers a development platform, enabling developers to build, test, and deploy applications without managing the underlying infrastructure. SaaS (Software as a Service) delivers software applications via the cloud, allowing users to access them over the internet without installation or maintenance concerns.</p></p>
