<p><p>The full glass curtain wall market comprises three main types based on glazing: single glazed, double glazed, and three glazed systems. Single glazed walls offer a lightweight and cost-effective option but provide limited thermal insulation. Double glazed systems enhance energy efficiency by incorporating two layers of glass with an insulated space in between, reducing heat transfer. Three glazed systems provide the highest insulation levels with three layers of glass, making them ideal for extreme climates, though they come at a higher cost.</p></p>
