<p><p>The hand dynamometer market is divided into two main types: electronic and mechanical medical dynamometers. Electronic medical dynamometers utilize advanced sensors and digital displays for precise strength measurements, often featuring data storage and analysis capabilities. They are designed for ease of use and accurate readings. In contrast, mechanical medical dynamometers rely on traditional spring mechanisms to measure grip strength through analog gauges, offering straightforward, cost-effective solutions for assessing muscle strength in clinical settings. Both types serve different user needs in rehabilitation and diagnostics.</p></p>
