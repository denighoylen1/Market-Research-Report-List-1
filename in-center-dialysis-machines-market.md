<p><strong>The In-center Dialysis Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li><li>Hemodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-center dialysis machines market is primarily divided into two types: peritoneal dialysis and hemodialysis. Peritoneal dialysis utilizes the patient's abdominal lining to filter waste, allowing for home-based treatment. In contrast, hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter blood outside the body, typically performed in a clinical setting. Both methods cater to patients with kidney failure, with hemodialysis being more prevalent in in-center facilities due to its immediate and controlled treatment environment.</p></p>
