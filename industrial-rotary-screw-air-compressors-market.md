<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Double Stage</li><li>Multistage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial rotary screw air compressors can be categorized into three main types based on their compression stages: single-stage, double-stage, and multistage. </p><p>Single-stage compressors compress air in one stage, making them efficient for lower pressure applications. Double-stage compressors use two stages for increased efficiency and higher pressure outputs, ideal for medium to heavy industrial use. Multistage compressors utilize several stages for maximum compression, suitable for high-demand applications requiring very high pressures, providing enhanced performance and reduced energy consumption.</p></p>
