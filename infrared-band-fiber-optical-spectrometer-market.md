<p><p>The Infrared Band Fiber Optical Spectrometer market is divided into two primary types: Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers and Multimode Fiber Spectrometers. Single Mode Fiber Spectrometers utilize thin fibers to transmit light for high-resolution spectroscopy, ideal for long-distance and precise measurements. In contrast, Multimode Fiber Spectrometers employ thicker fibers, allowing multiple light paths, which enhances signal strength and is suitable for shorter distances and broader spectral analysis. Both serve various applications across industries like environmental monitoring, medical diagnostics, and telecommunications.</p></p>
