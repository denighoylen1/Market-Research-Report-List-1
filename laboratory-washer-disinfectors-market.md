<p><ul><li>Cabinet(Single Chamber) Machines</li><li>Continuous Process Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory washer disinfectors are essential for maintaining hygiene in various settings, and they come in two main types: Cabinet (Single Chamber) Machines and Continuous Process Machines. Cabinet machines operate in a single chamber, effectively cleaning and disinfecting items in batch mode, making them suitable for smaller laboratories. In contrast, Continuous Process Machines facilitate a seamless flow of items through multiple stages of cleaning and disinfection, optimizing efficiency for larger laboratories with high throughput needs. Both types ensure strict compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
