<p><strong>The Medical Insufflator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"High Flow Insufflator"</li><li>"Middle Flow Insufflator"</li><li>"Low Flow Insufflator"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical insufflator market is categorized into three main types based on gas flow rates. High flow insufflators deliver gas at rates exceeding 20 liters per minute, ideal for rapid tissue distension in large surgical procedures. Middle flow insufflators offer a moderate flow of 5-20 liters per minute, suitable for various minimally invasive surgeries. Low flow insufflators operate below 5 liters per minute, beneficial for delicate procedures requiring precision and reduced risk of CO2 embolism. Each type serves distinct surgical needs.</p></p>
