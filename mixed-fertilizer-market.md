<p><p>The mixed fertilizer market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and solid. Liquid fertilizers are nutrient-rich solutions that are easily absorbed by plants, providing rapid nutrient delivery and targeted application. They are ideal for precision farming and often used in foliar sprays. Solid fertilizers, on the other hand, include granules and powders. They provide a slower, sustained release of nutrients, making them suitable for bulk application and enhancing soil quality over time. Both types cater to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
