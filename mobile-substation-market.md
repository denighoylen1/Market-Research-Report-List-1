<p><p>The mobile substation market is primarily categorized into High Voltage (HV) and Low/Medium Voltage (LV/MV) mobile substations. HV mobile substations are designed for high-voltage applications, providing quick and reliable power distribution during emergencies or maintenance. They are crucial for large-scale electrical systems. In contrast, LV/MV mobile substations cater to lower voltage requirements, ideal for smaller networks or temporary solutions in urban areas. Both types enhance grid flexibility and resilience while ensuring continuous power supply during outages or upgrades.</p></p>
