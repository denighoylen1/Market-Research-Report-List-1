<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC) market is categorized into two main types: passive and active PICs. Passive photonic integrated circuits primarily focus on guiding and manipulating light without any amplification, commonly used in applications like waveguides and filters. In contrast, active photonic integrated circuits incorporate components that can control and amplify optical signals, essential for functions such as modulation and detection. Both types are crucial for advancing technologies in telecommunications, data processing, and sensor applications, enhancing the efficiency of optical systems.</p></p>
