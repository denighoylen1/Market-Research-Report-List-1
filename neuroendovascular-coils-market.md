<p><p>The neuroendovascular coils market is primarily segmented into two types: bare platinum coils and coated bioactive coils. Bare platinum coils are traditional devices used for embolization of aneurysms, known for their versatility and effectiveness in occlusion. Coated bioactive coils, on the other hand, are designed with a polymer or bioactive material that enhances healing and promotes faster tissue integration, potentially reducing the risk of recanalization. Both types serve critical roles in the minimally invasive treatment of vascular conditions.</p></p>
