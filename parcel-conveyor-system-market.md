<p><p>The Parcel Conveyor System market primarily features two key types: roller conveyors and belt conveyors. Roller conveyors utilize a series of rollers to facilitate the movement of parcels, providing a smooth and efficient transport suitable for heavier loads. In contrast, belt conveyors employ a continuous loop of material to move parcels, ideal for lighter items and offering versatility in various settings. Both systems enhance logistics efficiency, streamline operations, and are crucial for automated material handling in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
