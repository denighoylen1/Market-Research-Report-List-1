<p><p>The patterned glass market primarily features two production methods: the Single Roller Method and the Double Roller Method. </p><p>The Single Roller Method involves pressing a glass sheet against a textured roller, creating a consistent pattern on one side while maintaining clarity on the other, suitable for decorative applications. </p><p>In contrast, the Double Roller Method uses two rollers to produce patterns on both sides, enhancing aesthetic appeal and functionality. This method often yields more intricate designs, making it popular for architectural and decorative purposes.</p></p>
