<p><strong>The PCB Buffer Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monorail Buffer Machine</li><li>Dual-rail Buffer Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB buffer machine market is segmented into two main types: monorail buffer machines and dual-rail buffer machines. Monorail buffer machines utilize a single track for the transportation and buffering of printed circuit boards, providing a streamlined and space-efficient solution ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, dual-rail buffer machines feature two parallel tracks, allowing for increased capacity and flexibility in high-volume production environments. Both types enhance workflow efficiency and minimize handling time within PCB manufacturing processes.</p></p>
