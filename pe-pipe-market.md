<p><ul><li>HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) Pipe</li><li>MDPE (Medium -Density Polyethylene) Pipe</li><li>LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PE pipe market includes three main types of polyethylene pipes: HDPE, MDPE, and LDPE. HDPE pipes are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications such as water supply and gas distribution. MDPE pipes offer a balance of flexibility and strength, suitable for gas and water systems with lower pressure requirements. LDPE pipes are lightweight and flexible, primarily used for low-pressure applications like irrigation and cable protection. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
