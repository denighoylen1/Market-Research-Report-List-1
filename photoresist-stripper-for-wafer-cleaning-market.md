<p><strong>The Photoresist Stripper for Wafer Cleaning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripper</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist stripper market for wafer cleaning is divided into two main types: positive and negative photoresist strippers. Positive photoresist strippers are designed to remove layers of positive photoresist, which becomes soluble upon exposure to light, facilitating precision in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, negative photoresist strippers are used to eliminate negative photoresist, where the exposed areas become less soluble. Both types are crucial for ensuring high-quality wafer surfaces, enhancing performance in photolithography processes used in microelectronics fabrication.</p></p>
