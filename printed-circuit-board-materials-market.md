<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) materials market is categorized primarily into photoresist, copper-clad laminate (CCL), glass fabric, and other materials. Photoresist is a light-sensitive material used in the etching process to create intricate circuit patterns. CCL is a core material that comprises a substrate with a copper layer, essential for electrical connections. Glass fabric serves as a reinforcing medium in PCBs, enhancing mechanical strength and thermal resistance. Other materials include various resins and conductive inks, catering to specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
