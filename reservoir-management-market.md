<p><p>Reservoir management can be categorized into two market types: static and dynamic models. Static models focus on long-term assessments, utilizing fixed parameters and historical data to optimize water storage and usage. In contrast, dynamic models incorporate real-time data and analytical techniques to adapt to changing conditions such as weather patterns and water demand. Both approaches aim to enhance resource efficiency and sustainability, but dynamic models offer greater flexibility to respond to immediate challenges and changing environmental factors.</p></p>
