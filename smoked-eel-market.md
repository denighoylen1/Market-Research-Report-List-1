<p><strong>The Smoked Eel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-smoke</li><li>Cold-smoke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoked eel market is divided into two primary types: hot-smoked and cold-smoked. Hot-smoked eel is cooked during the smoking process, resulting in a firmer texture and a more intense flavor, often favored for immediate consumption. Cold-smoked eel, on the other hand, is cured at lower temperatures, maintaining a delicate, buttery texture and a subtler taste, making it popular for gourmet dishes and charcuterie boards. Both types cater to distinct culinary preferences and market demands.</p></p>
