<p><p>The spectral filters market includes several key types: Dichroic filters selectively transmit specific wavelengths while reflecting others, often used in imaging and lighting applications. Neutral Density (ND) filters uniformly reduce intensity across the spectrum, ideal for controlling exposure in photography. Band-pass filters allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass, useful in spectroscopy and various optical systems. Other market segments may include high-pass, low-pass, and interference filters, catering to diverse applications in science, industry, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
