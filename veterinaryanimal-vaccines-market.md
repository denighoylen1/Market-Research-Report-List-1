<p><p>The veterinary/animal vaccines market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Live Attenuated Vaccines** use weakened pathogens to stimulate immunity without causing disease.</p><p>2. **Inactivated Vaccines** contain killed pathogens, providing a safer option but often requiring booster shots.</p><p>3. **Toxoid Vaccines** contain inactivated toxins to generate an immune response against specific diseases.</p><p>4. **Recombinant Vaccines** are genetically engineered to produce specific antigens, enhancing safety and efficacy.</p><p>5. **Other Vaccines** include various novel formulations and delivery methods not fitting into standard categories.</p></p>
